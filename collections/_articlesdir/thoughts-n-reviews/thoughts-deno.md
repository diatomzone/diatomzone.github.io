## what's it?!

kamen rider den-o is the 2007 installment of the long-running kamen rider series!!!

kamen rider is a kid's tokusatsu (live action with tons of special effects) show about a hero who transforms to fight evil, and every season features a new cast/premise/marketable gimmick {{site.data.emojis.zenigata}}

den-o is about a time-traveling train, our guy who's possessed by 4 color-coded demons, and coffee that looks like playdough?!

## diagetics headcanons

### are imagin possession effects diagetic?

(AKA do they exist in-universe, rather than only from the audience's perspective?)

on one hand, NPCs are baffled by changes in ryoutarou's behavior, but never indicate changes in physical appearance (eye color, hair, etc.)

on the other hand, possessed ryoutarou touches/poses with his glasses/hat, indicating physical objects.

and on the third, if the effects are like imagin sand, which goes unnoticed by NPCs but does visually alert the protagonists, then maybe only imagin knowers can see them.

(ACTUALLY in "episode blue" ryuutaros explicitly points out the sand to a civilian grandma, but i couldn't tell if she was just playing along with this bizarre kid who barged into her house uninvited.)

i think realistically the answer is "only visible to imagin knowers", but in my heart they're fully diagetic since that's the funniest option. these guys are so dedicated to their looks they're either materializing outfits through sheer force of will, or they're making ryoutarou carry 4(?) costumes at all times {{site.data.emojis.zenigata}}

(alternatively, "no" would mean urataros is miming wearing glasses which is also extremely silly)

### what are the main imagin's bodies made of?

typically enemy imagin have obvious fullbody themes and materials — metal armor, bird feathers, slimy jellyfish flesh, etc. — in comparison, the taros's are visually much more "guy in a suit".

their armor pieces are easy to explain, turtle shells or gold metal or whatever, but momotaros and urataros especially have a ton of exposed "body" between those pieces... so what is that supposed to be???

momotaros's face is skull-like and the pattern on his stomach resembles abdominal muscles, so i could see him being some kind of sinewy bone+muscle creature.

i don't actually know what urataros is supposed to be {{site.data.emojis.reigen}} presumably leathery turtle skin?

the way the somewhat loose material crinkles and pulls when the characters move and pose is SOOOOOO characteristic and visually impactful!!! especially on momotaros who's always doing crazy stuff with his legs. if their bodies were smooth/skintight like the den-o suit, it would be so nothing....

considering the designers could have easily used a more specific material, the fabric look is both vital and intentional! but i doubt these guys are meant to be "wearing clothes" in the same way kintaros wears a vest, or how ryuutaros and deneb are dressed in long coats, with deneb wearing loose pants (does he have a fully mechanical body underneath?)...

therefore, this conundrum continues to disproportionately confound me.

### can imagin move their mouths/make facial expressions?

almost certainly, but in my heart the answer is no because masked/monster suit acting has such a specific charm {{site.data.emojis.loveimages}}

- so the armor isn't a separate entity, it's part of their Actual Body that, while more durable than flesh, is still being slashed and crushed

### taros's relationship

it's not uncommon to assume the elder 3 see ryuutaros as some kind of little brother figure, but i see their collective bond as rid of any relationship implying superiority/inferiority — it is absolutely vital to me that the 4 of them stand as complete equals... and one of them just happens to be an 8-year-old with a gun LSDFGJSLDIFGJFF

verdict: the taros's are roommates (ryoutarou's body) first, coworkers second, and (eventually) best friends third! YIPPEEEEE (SIMULTANEOUS NESTED CATEGORIES)

### contract holder and imagin

regarding the relationship between contract holder and imagin, i feel like it's typical for people to sort any combination of characters into familial or romantic relationships... but i cannot think of a situation where "a secret third thing" isn't funnier and more interesting!!!

imagin show up somewhere between uncooperative and antagonistic yet they're obligate ride-or-dies because they will quite literally die if you do... you can't really hide or hold each other at arm's length because you have a psychic/telepathic bond, i can only assume you feel the force each other's emotions...

witnessing their personality front-row and gleaning the (distant, forgotten) past that shaped it by physically experiencing patterns of speech and movement that feel so foreign to your own body.... exchanging habits and body language through muscle memory, slowly growing more attuned and alike over time.... the presence of another in your mind changing from an intrusion to a reassurance....

overpowering others and being overpowered in a way completely unrelated to physical/human strength, or surrendering yourself in the face of mortal danger, knowing the friends you believe in will see things through.......

i guess the verdict is that it's deeply intimate (no romantic implication) in a really cool fantastical way!
